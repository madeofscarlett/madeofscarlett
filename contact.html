FAQ


1. How much do you charge?
I currently charge £40 (GBP; roughly $50 USD) per finished minute of audio. This rate includes two rounds of revisions - one for composition, and one for the final arrangement.

2. What styles do you do?
I work in a range of styles. At the moment, I mostly work in classical-adjacent, cinematic-sounding music like you might hear in Baldur's Gate or Hollow Knight, but I am also known to dabble in lots of other genres. Let me know - maybe I've been looking for an excuse to work in the style you like!

3. Where can I find examples of past praise/work?
I am collecting testimonials on the Commissions page of my website. You can see examples of my past work on the Media page.
